Our Mission

The Medina County Herb Society is dedicated to promoting the knowledge, use, and enjoyment of herbs. We strive to educate our community about the many benefits of herbs in gardening, cooking, health, and everyday life.

Our History

Founded by a group of passionate volunteers, the Medina County Herb Society has been serving the community for many years. What began as a small gathering of herb enthusiasts has grown into a vibrant organization that hosts educational programs, supports local gardens, and shares the joy of herbs with people of all ages.

What We Do

  • Monthly meetings with guest speakers and demonstrations
  • Community outreach and herb education programs
  • Maintaining and supporting local herb gardens
  • Sharing recipes, growing tips, and herbal traditions
  • Encouraging friendship and fellowship among members
